Communication Policy

At Washington University Nursery School, we believe that open and effective communication between parents and teachers is essential for your child's success.


Daily Communication

Teachers are available to speak with parents at drop-off and pick-up times. We encourage brief conversations during these times to share information about your child's day.

For more detailed discussions or concerns, please schedule a time to meet with your child's teacher outside of drop-off and pick-up hours.

Parent-Teacher Conferences

Formal parent-teacher conferences are scheduled twice per year (fall and spring) to discuss your child's progress, development, and any concerns. These conferences provide an opportunity for in-depth discussion about your child's learning and growth.

Additional conferences can be scheduled at any time by contacting your child's teacher or the school office.

Written Communication

The school uses various methods to keep parents informed:

  • Newsletters: Classroom newsletters are sent home regularly with updates about classroom activities and upcoming events.
  • Email: Important announcements and reminders are sent via email. Please ensure we have your current email address on file.
  • Bulletin Boards: Information is posted on classroom and school bulletin boards.
  • Parent Handbook: Comprehensive information about school policies and procedures is available in the Parent Handbook.

Emergency Communication

In case of emergencies or urgent situations, the school will contact parents immediately using the contact information provided on your child's enrollment form. Please notify the school office immediately if your contact information changes.

Contacting the School

  • Email: nursery@wustl.edu
  • In Person: The school office is open during regular school hours

For questions or concerns, please don't hesitate to contact us. We are here to support you and your child.
